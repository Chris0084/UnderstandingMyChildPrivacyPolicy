Privacy Policy – Understanding My Child

Last updated:15/02/2026

1. Overview

Understanding My Child helps parents and carers record observations and reflect on what may support their child’s development. We are committed to protecting privacy and complying with UK GDPR.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: email address and authentication details.
  • User-generated content: journal entries, notes, checklist selections.
  • Technical Information: basic device or crash data for functionality.

3. Special Category Data

Some information may relate to health or developmental characteristics. Processing is limited to providing core functionality and based on user consent.

4. How We Use Your Information

To create and maintain accounts, store entries securely, enable cross-device access, provide features, and maintain service performance. We do not sell data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using secure systems and encrypted connections with access controls.

6. Third-Party Services

Trusted providers may be used for authentication (Apple/Google), cloud hosting, and payment processing.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Sharing occurs only where necessary to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained while accounts remain active. Users may request deletion at any time.

9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access
  • Right to correction
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to withdrawal of consent

10. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features evolve.
