# Privacy Policy – Understanding My Child
**Last updated:** 15/02/2026
---

### 1. Overview
Understanding My Child helps parents and carers record observations and reflect on what may support their child’s development. We are committed to protecting privacy and complying with **UK GDPR**.

### 2. Information We Collect
* **Account Information:** Email address and authentication details.
* **User-generated content:** Journal entries, notes, and checklist selections.
* **Technical Information:** Basic device or crash data for functionality.

### 3. Special Category Data
Some information may relate to health or developmental characteristics. Processing is limited to providing core functionality and is based on user consent.

### 4. How We Use Your Information
To create and maintain accounts, store entries securely, enable cross-device access, provide features, and maintain service performance. **We do not sell data.**

### 5. Data Storage and Security
Data is stored using secure systems and encrypted connections with strict access controls.

### 6. Third-Party Services
Trusted providers may be used for:
* Authentication (Apple/Google)
* Cloud hosting
* Payment processing

### 7. Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data. Sharing occurs only where necessary to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

### 8. Data Retention
Data is retained while accounts remain active. Users may request deletion at any time.

### 9. Your Rights (UK GDPR)
You have the following rights regarding your data:
* Access and correction
* Deletion (the "right to be forgotten")
* Restriction of processing
* Withdrawal of consent

### 10. Children’s Privacy
The app is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children.

### 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy as features evolve. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
