# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 9, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the **Understanding My Child** mobile application, together with any related services operated by Christopher Ewart (collectively, the "Application"). Christopher Ewart is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".

---

## 1. Core Data Philosophy & Local Storage

Because the Application focuses on developmental journaling for children, the Service Provider prioritizes strict user privacy. **Absolutely no personal logs, journal text, child names, or user text/voice inputs are ever collected, transmitted, or seen by the Service Provider.** 

All your personal developmental journal entries are stored strictly locally on your physical device using secure local storage. 

---

## 2. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects certain information when you download and use it. This falls into two categories:

### A. Automatically Collected Usage Data (Analytics)
To help the Service Provider understand how users interact with the Application, improve stability, and diagnose crashes, the Application utilizes **Google Firebase Analytics**. 

This data is entirely aggregated and anonymized. It does not identify you personally. Collected analytics parameters include:
* Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address.
* The specific screens of the Application that you visit, along with the date, time, and duration of your visit.
* Your mobile operating system and device model.
* App-lifecycle events (e.g., app launches, crashes, and feature interaction).

### B. In-App Purchase Data
The Application utilizes **RevenueCat** to process and manage premium features or subscriptions. RevenueCat processes transactional information strictly to validate purchase tokens and sync subscription states. It does not have access to your personal journal entries.

---

## 3. Your Privacy Choices & Opt-Out Rights

The Service Provider believes in absolute user autonomy over tracking mechanisms. 

* **First-Launch Consent:** When you open the Application for the first time, you will be prompted with a clear pop-up banner to grant or deny permission for anonymous usage analytics. 
* **Changing Preferences at Any Time:** You are under no obligation to share usage data, and the Application will function identically regardless of your choice. You can opt-in or opt-out of anonymous analytics at any time by navigating to the **Settings Screen** (via the gear icon on the Home Screen) and toggling the **"Share Anonymous Usage Data"** switch.
* **Full Device Removal:** You can stop all automated information collection by uninstalling the Application from your mobile device.

## 5. Disclosure of Information

The Service Provider may disclose automatically collected or aggregated information only under the following circumstances:
* As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.
* When believed in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, protect user safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
* With trusted service providers who work strictly on behalf of the Service Provider, have no independent use of the information, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

## 6. International Data Transfers

The third-party service providers utilized (Google and RevenueCat) may transfer and store aggregated data in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. Where applicable law requires legal transfer safeguards (such as for residents of the European Economic Area), these providers utilize mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission or recognized adequacy decisions.

## 7. Data Retention Policy

* **User-Provided Data (Local Journal Entries):** Stored indefinitely on your physical device. The Service Provider does not host this data on external servers and cannot recover or delete it for you. Deleting the app clears this data locally.
* **Automatically Collected Analytics Data:** Anonymized usage tracking reports generated via Firebase are retained for up to 24 months from collection before automatic deletion, unless longer retention is required for legal compliance.
* **Aggregated Data:** Fully anonymized, structural app performance statistics may be retained indefinitely to assist with long-term app optimization.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Application is intended for use by parents, legal guardians, and adult caregivers tracking a child's developmental journey. It is not intended for direct use by children under 16 years of age. 

The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit or collect personally identifiable information from children under 16. In the event the Service Provider discovers that a child has bypassed app parameters and provided personal information directly, it will be deleted immediately. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately at jstarapps@yahoo.com.

## 9. Regional Rights & Regulations

### Your Rights (GDPR / UK Data Protection Act)
If you reside within the European Union or the United Kingdom, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or permanent erasure of any personal data processed under this policy, or to withdraw consent. Because your data is anonymized or kept strictly local, you can fully manage erasure by uninstalling the app or resetting local storage. 

### Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt-out of the sharing of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. The Service Provider does not "sell" your personal data. 

To exercise any of these regional rights, contact the Service Provider at **jstarapps@yahoo.com**.
